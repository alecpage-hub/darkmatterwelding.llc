About Dark Matter Welding

Hi, I'm Alec Page, founder of Dark Matter Welding. A year ago, I started this company with my best friend Martin Rivas, and it's been an incredible journey.

Martin and I met in welding school where we discovered we had more than just a passion for welding in common—we had a genuine friendship. Yeah, I may have burnt him during school (sorry about that!), but somehow that incident brought us closer together. We laughed it off, forgave each other, and realized we were meant to work together. I'm a veteran, proud to be married to my beautiful wife Stephanie for the past 3 years, and Martin is not just my business partner—he's family. He's been living with us, and we do everything together.

Dark Matter Welding was born from a simple idea: deliver exceptional welding and fabrication services with fun and genuine care. Stephanie has been incredibly supportive, and with Martin by my side, we've built something special. We're loud, we're fun, and we bring that energy to every project. Whether it's custom fabrication, structural welding, mobile services, or metal art, we approach every job with the same commitment to excellence and passion that started this whole thing.
